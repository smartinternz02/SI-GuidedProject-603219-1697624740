Detecting COVID-19 using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and the VGG16 architecture has emerged as a promising approach in the fight against the pandemic. CNNs are a type of deep learning algorithm that excels at identifying patterns in images, making them well-suited for analyzing chest X-rays and CT scans for signs of COVID-19. The VGG16 architecture, specifically, has been shown to achieve high accuracy in COVID-19 detection tasks.
